Signs that may say that there are parasites in your body

Infecting with parasites can be easier than it seems. Even a bite of a normal mosquito can turn into a serious problem: after contact with a sick animal, it can infect you with dirofilariasis. The main dangers of parasites for a body - metabolic disorders, intoxication of a body, anemia and allergic reactions.
Parasites include bacteria, viruses, fungi, and worms, protozoa, unicellular, arthropods, protozoal invasions and other species. The main thing that unites them - they eat at the expense of an "owner", damaging his health. And, if some parasites have microscopic dimensions, others grow to 10 meters and can live 20 years.
Some parasitic diseases do not have florid clinical symptoms but there are those that significantly impair the quality of life and even lead to death.
Each region is characterized by its parasitic diseases which are very closely related to climatic conditions. In countries with a warm and humid climate, there are much more parasites than in the temperate.
If you do not follow the basic rules of hygiene you can get infected with enterobiasis. Its symptoms are: severe itching in the anus, bad appetite, grating teeth in sleep, and fast fatigue.
If you eat raw fish, a parasite like a Diphyllobothrium latum, the length of which can reach 10 meters can get in a body. The main signs of the disease are: exhaustion of a body, anemia, frequent abdominal pain, morning vomiting, nausea, constant fatigue and drowsiness.
For each type of parasite is characteristic of its clinical manifestations but if there is an unreasonable temperature, including subfebrile, rashes on skin, poorly treatable, prolonged, recrudesce, obstructive bronchitis accompanied by shortness of breath, it is better to make a test or at least give a general blood test. If it increases the number of eosinophils that are responsible for allergic reactions then it is worth thinking about helminthic invasions.
Also, signs of the presence of parasites in the body can be abdominal pain, anemia, flatulence, diarrhea, a decrease or increase weight, bruxism (grinding teeth in a dream), decreased immunity.
